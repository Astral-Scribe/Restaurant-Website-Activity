A Hearth at the End of Every Quest

Est. Year of the Dragon, Age 412

The Gilded Flagon has stood at this crossroads longer than any map can confirm. Kings have eaten here. Beggars have eaten here. One wizard ate here three times in a single evening and refused to explain why. We do not ask. We serve.

The kitchen operates by a simple code: real ingredients, real fire, no shortcuts. The Innkeeper sources locally where possible, and from considerably further afield when necessary. Some suppliers prefer not to be named.
