House Rules

  • Parties of 8+ require 48 hours notice
  • No live familiars inside the dining room. The courtyard is available.
  • No spellcasting at the bar. The stools are still burned from the last time.
  • 10% surcharge on public holidays
  • Adventuring parties of four are seated faster. This is not a coincidence.
  • Please mention dietary requirements in your booking
  • The Innkeeper does not give directions to the dungeon. The Innkeeper has never heard of the dungeon. There is no dungeon.
  • The Innkeeper reserves the right to refuse service to anyone who was rude on their last visit, regardless of how long ago that was.
  • Pets welcome in the courtyard. Familiars welcome everywhere except Table 7. Table 7 knows why.
